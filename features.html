Powerful Features

Everything you need to combine and edit screenshots professionally

Core Features

Smart Alignment

Automatically snap screenshots together with pixel-perfect precision. Our intelligent alignment system detects edges and helps you create seamless combinations effortlessly.

Drawing Tools

Add annotations, highlights, arrows, and freehand drawings. Choose from multiple colors and brush sizes to emphasize important areas in your screenshots.

Text Annotation

Add text labels, captions, and explanations. Customize font family, size, weight, and color to match your style and improve clarity.

Image Cropping

Crop and trim images to focus on important areas. Precise visual controls with real-time preview. Confirm or cancel with keyboard shortcuts (Enter/Escape).

Background Customization

Choose transparent, solid color, or image backgrounds. Perfect for creating professional presentations or matching your brand colors.

100% Private

All processing happens locally in your browser. Your screenshots never leave your device, ensuring complete privacy and security.

Lightning Fast

No uploads, no waiting. Process unlimited screenshots instantly with our browser-based technology. Works offline too!

Upload & Import

Multiple ways to add your screenshots:

  • Click to Upload: Browse and select files from your computer
  • Drag & Drop: Drag files directly onto the canvas
  • Paste from Clipboard: Press ⌘V (Mac) or Ctrl+V (Windows) to paste screenshots instantly
  • Multiple Files: Upload multiple screenshots at once
  • Format Support: PNG, JPG, JPEG, GIF, WebP

Editing & Manipulation

Transform Tools

  • Move: Click and drag to reposition screenshots
  • Resize: Drag corner handles to scale proportionally
  • Rotate: Rotate screenshots to any angle
  • Flip Horizontal: Mirror screenshots left-to-right
  • Flip Vertical: Mirror screenshots top-to-bottom

Crop Tool

  • Precise Cropping: Select an image and click the crop button to enter crop mode
  • Visual Feedback: Semi-transparent overlay shows areas that will be removed
  • Adjustable Crop Area: Drag and resize the crop rectangle to select the exact area you want to keep
  • Keyboard Shortcuts: Press K to toggle crop mode, Enter to confirm, Escape to cancel
  • Multiple Crops: Crop the same image multiple times to refine your selection
  • Undo Support: Easily undo crop operations if you change your mind

Layer Management

  • Bring to Front: Move selected objects above others
  • Send to Back: Move selected objects behind others
  • Overlap Control: Create complex layered compositions

Navigation & View

  • Zoom In/Out: Get close for detailed work or zoom out for overview
  • Fit to Screen: Automatically adjust view to see entire canvas
  • Pan Canvas: Hold spacebar and drag to navigate large compositions
  • Zoom Percentage: See current zoom level at a glance

History & Undo

  • Unlimited Undo: Go back through your entire editing history
  • Redo: Restore actions you've undone
  • Keyboard Shortcuts: Ctrl+Z to undo, Ctrl+Y to redo
  • Non-Destructive: Experiment freely without losing work

Export & Share

  • High-Quality PNG: Export with transparency support
  • Copy to Clipboard: Paste directly into other applications
  • Quick Download: Save to your device with one click
  • No Watermarks: Your images, completely clean

Explore Use Cases

PictriKit is more than a screenshot tool — it's a visual expression tool for organizing and communicating ideas.

Create Tutorial Images

A tutorial image combines multiple screenshots with annotations to explain a process. Instead of sending 5 separate images, you create one clear visual that shows the complete workflow from start to finish.

When to Use:
  • Explaining software features to colleagues
  • Creating documentation for users
  • Writing how-to blog posts
  • Onboarding new team members

Document Product Feedback

Text descriptions of bugs are often misunderstood. A screenshot with annotations shows exactly what's wrong, where it happened, and what you expected. This saves time for both reporters and developers.

When to Use:
  • Reporting bugs with reproduction steps
  • Suggesting UI improvements
  • Comparing expected vs actual behavior
  • Documenting issues for QA teams

Organize Your Thoughts

Sometimes ideas exist as fragments — a screenshot here, a note there, a reference image somewhere else. Combining them into one visual helps you see connections, identify gaps, and communicate your thinking to others.

When to Use:
  • Planning a project or feature
  • Comparing multiple options
  • Documenting research findings
  • Preparing for a discussion or meeting

Present Design Concepts

Describing a design idea in words is hard. Showing a combination of reference images makes your vision instantly clear to clients, teammates, or AI tools.

When to Use:
  • Pitching design ideas to clients
  • Creating mood boards for projects
  • Explaining concepts to AI design tools
  • Collaborating with remote teams
